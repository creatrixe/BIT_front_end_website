WandrPass for Restaurants, Breweries and Bars in Vancouver

With so many friends in the local hospitality industry, we created WandrPass to help these businesses and the people who depend on them.

WandrPass is a mobile application that helps the best local restaurants, breweries and bars attract new customers by hosting exclusive promotional offers called WandrPasses.

Here's how the app works

Download the Merchant App

Download the WandrPass merchant app, make your business profile, and set up your first WandrPass

Scan your customer QR codes

Redeem customer WandrPasses by scanning their QR code at your POS and input their bill price into the app

Use the Merchant Web Portal to help make the best decision for your business

You are in control - add, edit, or remove WandrPass offers whenever you’d like and see just how effective WandrPass is at attracting new customers and helping you upsell!
